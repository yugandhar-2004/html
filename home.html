Weather Dashboard

Current Weather Section

This section provides information about the current weather conditions, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and general weather status like sunny, rainy, or cloudy.

Users can get real-time updates on what the weather is like at their location or another selected place.

5-Day Forecast Section

The 5-day forecast section displays the upcoming weather trends for the next five days. It shows temperatures, expected precipitation, and other weather details.

This helps users plan ahead, whether they are looking at daily temperature ranges or rain predictions for the next few days.

Weather Map Section

The weather map shows a graphical representation of weather patterns, such as high and low-pressure systems, rain zones, and wind directions.

This map helps users visualize where weather changes are happening and what areas are affected by different weather phenomena.

Geocoding Section

This section allows users to find weather information based on geographical coordinates or a specific location, like a city or country.

Users can input their current location or search for weather data in other places around the world.

Air Pollution Section

The air pollution section provides information about the air quality in a specific area. It shows the levels of pollutants like PM2.5 and PM10, and the overall Air Quality Index (AQI).

This section helps users understand how clean or polluted the air is and whether it is safe for outdoor activities.
